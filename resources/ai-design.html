Best Practices

1. Use AI as a Starting Point

Don't rely solely on AI output. Use it to generate ideas and concepts, then refine with human creativity and judgment.

2. Maintain Brand Consistency

Train AI tools on your brand guidelines to ensure generated content aligns with your visual identity.

3. Iterate and Refine

AI-generated designs are rarely perfect on the first try. Use iterative prompting to refine results.

4. Combine Multiple Tools

Different AI tools excel at different tasks. Use ChatGPT for copy, Midjourney for visuals, and Figma AI for layouts.
