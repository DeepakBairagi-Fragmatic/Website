Healthcare Portal

Delivered a secure, responsive platform for patient data.

Challenge

The client needed a HIPAA-compliant portal that allowed patients to access their medical records securely. The existing system was outdated, not mobile-friendly, and difficult for elderly patients to use.

Solution

We designed and developed a secure, encrypted portal with a mobile-first approach. We prioritized accessibility, ensuring high contrast and large touch targets. The interface was simplified to guide users effortlessly to their appointments, prescriptions, and test results.

Results

The new portal achieved 99.9% uptime and received 5-star user feedback for its ease of use. Patient engagement with their health data increased significantly, leading to better health outcomes.
