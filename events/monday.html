Workshop Breakdown: UI Design Bootcamp

  1. 10:00 AM
    Introduction & Welcome

    Meet the facilitators and get an overview of today’s project and tools.

  2. 10:10 AM
    Color, Typography & Spacing Fundamentals

    Core design principles and establishing a purplewave color palette in Figma.

    Download Style Guide
  3. 10:40 AM
    From Figma to Tailwind

    Step-by-step: Export UI elements and implement them with Tailwind CSS. Quick demo of using the purple gradient utility classes.

  4. 11:00 AM
    Interactive Website Build

    Work in pairs: translate a Figma design into a working, responsive page using Tailwind. Mentors walk the room for on-the-spot feedback.

    Live Collab
  5. 11:20 AM
    Wrap-up & Q&A

    Final project walk-through, troubleshooting, and open Q&A with the facilitators.
    Get a chance to win PurpleWave swag in the feedback draw!

What to Bring:

  • Laptop or tablet (with Chrome/Firefox)
  • Free Figma account
  • GitHub or CodePen for live collaboration (optional)
  • Enthusiasm and your best purple vibes!
